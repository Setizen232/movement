Pay Per
Click Advertising?

Pay per click advertising (also known as search engine marketing) appears at the top and right side of a search engine results page (SERP).

Search engine marketing services work by associating your ad with a keyword or phrase related to your product or service. Any time that keyword is used in a search, your PPC advertisement will be shown on the search results page. You only pay for PPC advertising when a customer actually clicks the link to visit your website
